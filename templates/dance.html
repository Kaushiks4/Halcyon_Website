Classical Solo

RULES

PRELIMS:
  • Maximum of 3 participants are allowed per college.
  • The participants will be given a max of 3+2 mins on stage.
  • CD for dance should be made available to the concerned co-coordinator 3 hours prior to the event in a ready to play condition. (Only CD’s /USB in MP3 format)
  • Any costumes, gestures and actions should not be obscene.
  • Live band performance or group song accompanied by dancing is not allowed.
  • Poor CD quality is not the responsibility of authorities.
  • Any injury sustained by the participant while performing the dance is not the responsibility of the organizers.
  • The decision of the JUDGES is final and binding on all the participants.

DUET

RULES

  • Maximum three teams per college.
  • No gender restriction on team constitution.
  • Live band performance not allowed.
  • CD/USB to be provided in MP3 format only.
  • Time limit: 4+2 minutes.
Choreography

RULES

  • Maximum 3 teams per college.
  • Duration of performance: 6+2 min.
  • Pre-recorded CD is allowed on stage (MP3 format).
  • Recording should be on CD in ready to play condition which should be handed over 1 hour before the event.
  • Points will be awarded for costumes, interpretation and co-ordination with the music and group synchronization, entry – exit and facial expression.
  • Max no. of participants is 12 and minimum is 4.
  • Poor CD quality is not the responsibility of the authorities.
  • All participants should report to back stage 30 mins prior to beginning of the event.
  • Any injuries sustained by the participants while performing the dance are not the responsibilities of the organizers.
  • The decision of the JUDGES is final and binding.
