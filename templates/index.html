What's Halcyon?


‘Halcyon’, the summer fest of Siddaganga Institute of Technology is the largest cultural event that SIT hosts, held for a span of 3 days. Since its inception in 1992, the charm of this annual festival has anything but not diminished with the spirits of the participants, organizers and the audience growing stronger and bigger every year.

2500+ participants are taking part in the events of the fest. A fest of colours and creativity, Halcyon has always promised ecstatic performances and fun. A in the crown of SIT’s cultural events, Halcyon will be celebrating its 27th year of existence. Halcyon organizes a plethora of events in many genres and is a host to participants from various colleges of Karnataka.

Halcyon’20 returns with a bigger, better and louder bang!!! With a mind boggling 36+ events spread over 6 fabulous genres and some informal events, Halcyon’19 brings with it psyche performance and all the fun. We are giving away a whopping total of Rs 2,50,000+ cash prizes to the winners of the events.
